# __Module 1: Foundational Air Quality Concepts__
This module is a part of 5 AQE - Air Quality and Exposure

## __Course details__
Establishes the target variables for measurement and modeling, linking them directly to regulatory standards and health effects.
- Duration: 2 sessions; approx. 2 hrs each 

## __Sub-topics__
- Pollutant types (particles, gases): physical/chemical properties, primary/secondary distinction, health and environmental impacts
- WHO air quality guidelines, EU legislation, example of implementation - Croatia
  
## __Learning Outcomes__
After participating in the workshops and using the recommended literature, the participant of this module will be able to:
- Identify and categorize major atmospheric pollutants based on their chemical and physical properties. 
- Explain the difference between primary and secondary pollutants and their formation mechanisms. 
- Relate pollutant concentrations to established regulatory thresholds (WHO guidelines, EU Directives) and human health risks.
